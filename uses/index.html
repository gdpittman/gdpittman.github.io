Uses

In the spirit of uses.tech and the App Defaults pages that Robb Knight compiled following episode 097 of the Hemispheric Views podcast, this is a list of things I use on a semi-regular basis.

Hardware

  • Personal: 2024 MacBook Pro, M4 Pro, 1TB storage, 24GB memory
  • Work: 2023 M2 MacBook Air, 256 GB storage, 8GB memory
  • M2 iPad Pro 12.9", 1TB storage, 16GB memory
  • M4 iPad Pro 11", 512GB storage, 8GB memory
  • Magic Keyboard, 1st gen (it works perfectly fine with the M2 iPad Pro)
  • Magic Keyboard for iPad Pro 11-inch (M4)
  • iPhone 16 Pro Max, 512GB storage
  • Apple Watch Ultra
  • AirPods Pro, 2nd gen.
  • AirPods Max (very rarely; they essentially collect dust)

Software/Apps

  • Browser: Vivaldi (and, as needed, Safari)
  • Calendar: Fantastical
  • Camera: Camera (iOS) (occasionally Halide)
  • Contacts: Contacts (Apple) and Cardhop
  • Email: Proton (personal) and Outlook (for work, most unfortunately)
  • Messaging: Messages (Apple)
  • Music: Marvis Pro (for Apple Music) and Qobuz
  • Navigation: Magic Earth (a surprisingly strong privacy-first navigation app) and Apple Maps Waze
  • Notes: Obsidian (occasionally Apple Notes as needed)
  • Passwords: 1Password
  • Photos: Photos (Apple)
  • Podcasts: Overcast
  • RSS: Tapestry by Iconfactory
  • Social Media: Bluesky and Ivory for Mastodon
  • Storage: iCloud Drive and Proton Drive
  • Task Management: OmniFocus

Every Day Carry Analog Items

  • Pentel Quicker Clicker pencil
  • Tūl Discbound Notebook and LEUCHTTURM1917 notebook
  • Various fountain pens
